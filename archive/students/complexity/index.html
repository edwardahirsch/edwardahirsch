Semester II. Structural Cryptography.

The lecture notes are put on a "post-moderating" basis, that is, they are posted mostly unedited right when received. The dates point to the last substantial changes.

    TeX source, Postscript file, compressed Postscript file, PDF file.

    TeX source, Postscript file, compressed Postscript file, PDF file.
    One-way functions from pseudo-random generators. Pseudo-random generators from length-preserving one-way permutations.
